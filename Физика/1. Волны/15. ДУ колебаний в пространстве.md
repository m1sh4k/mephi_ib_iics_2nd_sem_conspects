$f(\omega t-kx)$

Производные по координате

$\displaystyle\frac{\delta f}{\delta x}=\frac{\delta f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)}\cdot \frac{\delta(\omega t+kx)}{\delta x}=\frac{\delta f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)}\cdot k$

$\displaystyle \frac{\delta f}{\delta x^{2}}=\frac{k\delta^{2}f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)^{2}}\cdot \frac{\delta(\omega t+kx)}{dx}=k^{2}\cdot \frac{d^{2}f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)^{2}}$

Производные по времени

$\displaystyle \frac{\delta f}{\delta t}=\frac{\delta f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)}\cdot \frac{\delta(\omega t+kx)}{\delta t}=\frac{\delta f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)}\cdot \omega$

$\displaystyle \frac{\delta f}{\delta t^{2}}=\omega^{2}\cdot \frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta(\omega t+kx)^{2}}$

$$
\begin{align*}

&\displaystyle \frac{1}{k^{2}}\cdot \frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta x^{2}}= \frac{1}{\omega^{2}}\cdot \frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta t^{2}} \\
&c:=\frac{\omega}{k} \\
&\boxed{\frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta t}-c^{2}\cdot \frac{\delta^{2}f}{\delta x}=0}
\end{align*}
$$

# 2  Пространственное уравнение

$f=A\cos(\omega t-kx)$

$\boxed{\lambda=\frac{\omega t}{k}}$

$\boxed{k=\frac{2\pi}{\lambda}}$

$\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}=k_{x}x+k_{y}y+k_{z}z$
$f(x,y,z,t)=f(\omega t+\vec{k}\cdot\vec{r})$

$\displaystyle \frac{\delta f}{\delta x}=\frac{\delta f}{d(\omega t+\vec{k}\vec{r})}=\omega \cdot \frac{df}{d(\omega t+{\vec{k}}\vec{v})}$
$\displaystyle \frac{d^{2}f}{dt^{2}}=\omega^{2} \frac{d^{2}f}{d(\omega t+\vec{k}\vec{r})^{2}}$

$\displaystyle \frac{d^{2}f}{dx^{2}}+\frac{d^{2}f}{dy^{2}}+\frac{d^{2}f}{dz^{2}}=\frac{d^{2}f}{d(\omega t+\vec{k}\vec{r})^{2}}\cdot(k_x^{2}+k_{y}^{2}+k_{z}^{2})=\frac{d^{2}f}{d(\omega t+\vec{k}\vec{r})^{2}}\cdot k^{2}$

$\displaystyle \frac{1}{\omega^{2}}\cdot \frac{d^{2}f}{dt^{2}}-\frac{1}{k^{2}}\bigtriangleup f=0$

$\boxed{\frac{d^{2}f}{dt^{2}}-c^{2}\bigtriangleup f=0}$
