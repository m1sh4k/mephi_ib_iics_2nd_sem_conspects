## Утв1

$f(x)\in\mathcal{R}[a;b],\quad[c;d]\subset[a;b]\implies f(x)\in\mathcal{R}[c;d]$

### Докво

$\displaystyle \forall\varepsilon>0\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}>0:\forall p_{[a;b]}\quad\lambda_{p}<\delta_{\varepsilon}\implies\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\omega(f,\Delta_{j})$ (по $[a;b]$)

$\forall\Pi$ разбиение $[c;d],\lambda_{\Pi},\delta_{\varepsilon}$

Дополним $\Pi$ до разбиения $[a;b]$ так, что добавленные точки на $[a;b]\setminus[c;d]$

$\displaystyle\varepsilon>\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\omega(f\Delta _{j})\Delta x_{j}\geq \sum_{j:x_{j}\in[c;d]}\omega(f,\Delta _{j})\Delta x_{j}\implies f(x)\in\mathcal{R}[c;d]$

## Утв2

$a<c<b,\quad f(x)\in\mathcal{R}[a;c]$
$f(x)\in \mathcal{R}[c;b]\implies f(x)\in\mathcal{R}[a;b]$ и 
$\displaystyle \int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx=\int_{a}^{x}f(x)dx+\int_{c}^{b}f(x)dx$

### Докво (проверка критерия Дарбу)

#### Ограниченность

$$
\begin{align*} 
\left.
\begin{aligned}
f(x)\in B[c;b] \\
f(x)\in B[a;c]
\end{aligned} \\
\right\}
f(x)\in B[a;b]
\end{align*}
$$

#### Предел

$$
\begin{align*}
&\forall\varepsilon>0\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}'>0\forall p_{[a;c]}\quad\lambda_{p\ [a;c]}<\delta_{\varepsilon}'  \\
&\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}\ [a;c]}\omega(f,\Delta_{j})\Delta x_{j}<\varepsilon \\
&\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}'':\forall p_{[a;b]}\quad \lambda_{p\ [a;b]}<\delta_{\varepsilon}'' \\
&\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}\ [c;b]}\omega(f,\Delta_{j})\Delta x_{j}<\varepsilon
\end{align*}
$$

$\delta_{\varepsilon}:=\min\{ \varepsilon,\delta_{\varepsilon}',\delta_{\varepsilon}'' \}>0$

$\displaystyle \forall p_{[a;b]}\quad\lambda_{p\ [a;b]}<\delta_{\varepsilon}$

$\displaystyle \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}\ [a;b]}\omega(f,\Delta Pj)\Delta x_{j}=\sum^{}_{j:\Delta_{j}\subset[a;c)}'+\sum_{j:\Delta_{j}\cap c\neq \emptyset}''+\sum_{j=\Delta_{j}\subset(c;b]}'''<\varepsilon$
