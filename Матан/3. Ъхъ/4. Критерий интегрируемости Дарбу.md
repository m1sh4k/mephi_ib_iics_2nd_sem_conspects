## Теорема 1

$f(x)\in B[a;b]$

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x)\in \mathcal{R}[a;b]\Longleftrightarrow  \lim\limits_{ \lambda_{p} \to 0 } (S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p})=0
\end{align*}
$$

### Докво $\implies$

$f(x)\in \mathcal{R}[a;b]$
$\exists I:\forall\varepsilon>0\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}>0:\forall p,\lambda_{p}<\delta_{\varepsilon},\forall \xi \quad |\sigma (f,p,\xi)-I|<\varepsilon\Longleftrightarrow I-\varepsilon <\sigma(f,p,\xi)<I+\varepsilon$

$I-\varepsilon\leq \inf\limits_{\xi}\sigma(f,p,\xi)=\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}\leq S_{p}=\sup\limits_{\xi}(f,p,\xi)\leq I+\varepsilon$
$I-\varepsilon\leq \overline{\overline{s}}_{p}\leq S_{p}\leq I+\varepsilon\Longleftrightarrow\boxed{0\leq S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}\leq{2}\varepsilon} \Longleftrightarrow \lim\limits_{ \lambda_{p} \to 0 }(S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p})=0$

### Докво $\Longleftarrow$

$\lim\limits_{ \lambda_{p} \to 0 } (S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p})=0$ 

$S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}\geq I^{*}-I_{*}\geq 0$

При $\lambda_{p}\to 0$ остается только одно число $\implies I^{*}=I_{*}=:I$

$\forall\varepsilon>0\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}>0:\forall p\quad \lambda_{p}<\lambda\delta_{\varepsilon}\quad 0\leq S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}<\varepsilon$

$\forall \xi \quad \overline{\overline{s}}_{p}\leq\sigma(f,p,\xi)\leq S_{p}\implies |\sigma(f,p,\xi)-I|\leq S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}<\varepsilon$

$\displaystyle \exists\lim\limits_{ \lambda_{p} \to 0 }\sigma(f,p,\xi)=I\implies f(x )\in \mathcal{R}[a;b]$ по определению

## Следствие

$\displaystyle S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}=\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}} \omega(f,\Delta_{j})\Delta x_{j}\to 0$ при $\lambda_{p}\to0$
