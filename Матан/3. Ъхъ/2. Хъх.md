
## Колебания

$$
\begin{align*}
 \omega(f,[a;b])=\sup_{[a;b]}f(x)-\inf_{[a;b]}f(x)
\end{align*}
$$

### Докво

$$
\begin{align*}
&\forall x',x''\in[a;b] \\
&f(x')-f(x'')\leq \sup_{[a;b]}f(x)-\inf_{[a;b]}f(x)\implies \sup(f(x')-f(x''))=\omega(f,[a;b])\leq M_{[a;b]}-m_{[a;b]}
\end{align*}
$$

## 3. Точность

$\forall\varepsilon>0$
$\exists x_{\varepsilon}':f(x'_{\varepsilon})>M-\dfrac{\varepsilon}{2}$
$\exists x''_{\varepsilon}\in[a;b]:f(x''_{\varepsilon})<m+\dfrac{\varepsilon}{2}$
$f(x_{\varepsilon}')-f(x_{\varepsilon}'')<\left( M-\dfrac{\varepsilon}{2} \right)-\left( m+\dfrac{\varepsilon}{2} \right)=M-m-\varepsilon$

## 4. Разность сумм Дарбу

$\displaystyle S_{p}-\overline{\overline{s}}_{p}=\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\omega(f,\Delta_{j})\Delta x_{j}$
