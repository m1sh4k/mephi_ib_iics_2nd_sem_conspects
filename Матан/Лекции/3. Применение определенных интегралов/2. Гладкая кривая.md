## Гладкая кривая

$$
\begin{align*}
\gamma: \left\{
\begin{aligned}
x=x(t) \\
y=y(t)
\end{aligned} 
\right.
\end{align*}
$$
На $[a;b]$:
1. $x(t),y(t)\in C^{1}[a;b]$
2. $\dot{x}^{2}+\dot{y}^{2}>0$

## Длина гладкой кривой

длина $\gamma$ = $\displaystyle \int_{a}^{b}\sqrt{ \dot{x}^{2}+\dot{y}^{2} }\ dt$

### Докво

$\displaystyle I:=\int_{a}^{b}\sqrt{ \dot{x}^{2}+\dot{y}^{2} }\ dt$
 $\forall p$ отрезка $[a;b]$ сходится

$$
\begin{align*}
&\forall p=p_{[a;b]} \\
&\bar{\bar{s}}_{p}^{\star}:=\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\sqrt{ (\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|)^{2} +(\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}| ^{2})}\ \Delta t_{j}\leq \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\inf\sqrt{ \dot{x}^{2}+\dot{y}^{2} }\ \Delta t_{j}\leq \bar{\bar{s}}_{p} \leq I\leq S_{p}=\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\sup\sqrt{ \dot{x}^{2}+\dot{y}^{2} }\ \Delta t_{j}\leq \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\sqrt{ (\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|)^{2} +(\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}| ^{2})}\ \Delta t_{j}=:S_{p}^{\star} \\
&\bar{\bar{s}}_{p}^{\star}\leq \bar{\bar{s}}\leq I\leq S_{p}\leq S_{p}^{\star} \\ \\
&\left\{
\begin{aligned}
x=x(t) \\
y=y(t)
\end{aligned}
\right. \\
&t\in\Delta_{j} \\
&\hline \\
&\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\sqrt{ (\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|)^{2} +(\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}| ^{2})}\ \Delta t_{j}\leq длина\ \gamma= \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}длина\ \gamma_{j}\leq  \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\sqrt{ (\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|)^{2} +(\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}| ^{2})}\ \Delta t_{j}
\end{align*}
$$

$I$ и длина $\gamma$ — числа, которые не зависят от разбиения.

$I$, длина $\gamma \in[\bar{\bar{s}}_{p}^{\star};S_{p}^{\star}]$ если $S_{p}^{\star}-\bar{\bar{s}}_{p}^{\star}\xrightarrow{\lambda_{p}\to0}0$
Теперь доказываем это

$$
\begin{align*}
&0\leq S_{p}^{\star}-\bar{\bar{s}}_{p}^{\star}=\sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\left[\sqrt{ (\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|)^{2} +(\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}| ^{2})}\ \Delta t_{j}-\sqrt{ (\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|)^{2} +(\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}| ^{2})}\ \right]\Delta t_{j}\leq  \\
&\leq \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\left( \sqrt{ \left( \max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|-\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|   \right)^{2} +\left( \max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}|-\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}|   \right)^{2} } \right)\ \Delta t_{j} \leq  \\
&\leq \sum_{j=1}^{n_{p}}\left[ \max_{\Delta_{j}  }|\dot{x}| -\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{x}|+\max_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}|-\min_{\Delta_{j}}|\dot{y}|\right] \Delta t_{j}
\end{align*}
$$

Берем произвольную епсилон

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall p,\lambda_{p}<\delta_{\varepsilon},\forall\varepsilon>0\quad\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}>0:\forall t',t''\in[a;b] \quad
|t'-t''|<\delta_{\varepsilon}\implies  
\left\{  
\begin{aligned}
&|\dot{x}(t')-\dot{x}(t'')|<\varepsilon \\
&|\dot{y}(t')-\dot{y}(t'')|<\varepsilon
\end{aligned}
\right.
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
\boxed{длина\ \gamma=\int_{a}^{b}\sqrt{ (\dot{x}(t))^{2}+(\dot{y}(t))^{2} }\ dt}
