- $\displaystyle M_{x}:=\sup_{[a;b]}|x(t)|$
- $\displaystyle M_{y}:=\sup_{[a;b]}|y(t)|$
- $\displaystyle m_{x}:=\inf_{[a;b]}|x(t)|$
- $\displaystyle m_{y}:=\inf_{[a;b]}|y(t)|$

$\sqrt{ m_{x}^{2}+m_{y}^{2} }\cdot(b-a)\leq$ длина $\gamma\leq \sqrt{ M_{x}^{2} +M_{y}^{2}}\cdot(b-a)$
