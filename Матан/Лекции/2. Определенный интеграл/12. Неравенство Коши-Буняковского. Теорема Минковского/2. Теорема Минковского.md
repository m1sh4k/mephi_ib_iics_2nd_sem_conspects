
$\displaystyle ||f||:=\left( \int_{a}^{b}f^{2}(x)dx \right)^{1/2}$ —  норма функции $f$

## Теорема Минковского

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x),g(x)\in \mathcal{R} [a;b]\implies \left( \int_{a}^{b}(f(x)+g(x))^{2} \right) ^{1/2}\leq  \left(  \int_{a}^{b}f^{2}(x)dx \right) ^{1/2} +\left( \int_{a}^{b}g^{2}(x)dx \right) ^{1/2}
\end{align*}
$$
^minkovsky-theorem

или

$$
||f+g||\leq ||f||+||g||
$$
^minkovsky-theorem-short
$$
||f+g||\leq ||f||+||g||
$$
### Докво (непосредственная проверка)
$$
\begin{align*}
&||f+g||^{2}\leq ||f||^{2}+2||f||\cdot ||g||+||g||^{2} \\
&\cancel{ ||f||^{2} }+2\int_{a}^{b}f(x)g(x)dx+\cancel{ ||g||^{2} }\leq \cancel{ ||f||^{2} }+2||f||\cdot ||g|| +\cancel{ ||g||^{2} } \\
&\int_{a}^{b}f(x)g(x)dx\leq ||f||\cdot ||g|| \\ 
&\left( \left( \int_{a}^{b}f(x)g(x)dx \right) ^{2} \right) ^{1/2}\leq \left(  \int_{a}^{b}f^{2}(x)dx\right)^{1/2}  \cdot \left( \int_{a}^{b}g^{2}(x)dx \right)^{1/2}  \\
&\left( \int_{a}^{b}f(x)g(x)dx \right) ^{2}\leq  \int_{a}^{b}f^{2}(x)dx\cdot \int_{a}^{b}g^{2}(x)dx 
\end{align*}
$$

В итоге получили [[1. Неравенство Коши-Буняковского#^unequality-cuachy-bunyakovsky|неравенство Коши-Буняковского]], значит утверждение верно.
